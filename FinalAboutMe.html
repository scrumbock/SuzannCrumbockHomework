Please allow me to introduce myself...

I am a visual storyteller - distilling content and images through the mediums of broadcast television and web.

I'm a Philadelphia based producer and web developer/designer. My work has radiated through dozens of cable network clients including Fox, TLC, Discovery, HGTV and Food Network, and has aired in over 20 countries.

I've brought the stories of mothers with high-risk pregnancies, families struggling to keep their restaurants open, and high end home improvements to millions watching through their flatscreens and laptop screens.

My only goal is to take your story/idea/product from crude concept to engaging content that will ignite the attention of the masses.

When I'm not crafting digital stories and websites, I can be found baking, running, and learning how to appreciate fine whiskies.
